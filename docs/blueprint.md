# **App Name**: LeadLearn

## Core Features:

- Personalized Student Dashboard: Displays individual readiness scores, active assignments, study streak tracker, and a live class activity feed, all customized to the student's progress.
- Topic & Role Management: Allows students to manage their assigned topics and associated roles (e.g., Lead Presenter, Peer Reviewer), with detailed progress tracking for each.
- AI-Powered Socratic Tutor: An interactive AI tool that fosters deep understanding by challenging students with Socratic questions, progressively adjusting difficulty based on their responses, rather than providing direct answers.
- AI-Driven Knowledge Gap Analysis & Resources: Utilizes AI to analyze quiz performance and chat history, identify student strengths and weaknesses, and curate personalized learning resources tailored to specific knowledge gaps.
- Teacher Insights Dashboard: Provides educators with a comprehensive overview of student and class readiness, flagging at-risk students, highlighting common knowledge gaps across the class, and offering AI-driven instructional suggestions.
- Automated Gamification System: A system for tracking and rewarding student engagement through experience points (XP) for various activities, awarding badges for milestones, and featuring a class-wide leaderboard to encourage participation and consistent study habits.
- Pre-Class Artifact Submission & Analysis: Enables students to submit prepared learning artifacts like summaries or discussion questions. An AI tool analyzes these submissions for comprehension, identifies potential gaps, and updates their readiness score.

## Style Guidelines:

- Scheme: A dark, modern interface. The primary color is a vibrant purplish-blue (#6C63FF), signifying intelligence and dynamic action, used for key interactive elements and strong visual highlights. The background is a very dark, subtly bluish-grey (#0A0A0F) to provide a deep canvas, enhancing content focus. A striking pink (#FF6584) serves as an accent color, providing high contrast and an energetic, bold touch for alerts and secondary highlights, complementing the primary blue.
- Headline and main title elements leverage 'Bebas Neue' (sans-serif) for a commanding, contemporary, and educational aesthetic. Body text and most UI components use 'DM Sans' (sans-serif) for excellent legibility and a clean, approachable feel. Monospace elements like timestamps and XP displays employ 'DM Mono' (monospace) for structured, data-focused clarity.
- A curated set of modern, flat, emoji-style icons (e.g., ⚡, 📚, 🤖) are used throughout the application. These icons maintain visual consistency, simplify navigation, and contribute to an engaging yet minimalistic aesthetic.
- The application employs a fixed left-hand sidebar for persistent navigation, paired with a dynamic, responsive main content area. Information is logically organized into card-based components within flexible grid layouts (2-column, 3-column), ensuring a clear visual hierarchy and adaptive experience across different screen sizes.
- Subtle and purposeful animations are integrated to enhance the user experience. This includes 'fadeIn' transitions for content and pages, pulsating effects ('xpPulse', 'pulse') for gamification elements and live indicators, and 'modalIn' for smooth, attention-grabbing modal dialogs, providing a responsive and fluid interaction without being intrusive.
